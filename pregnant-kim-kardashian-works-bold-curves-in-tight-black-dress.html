    02 Sep 2024, 00:00

    She’s back in black! Pregnant Kim Kardashian slipped her blossoming baby bump into one of her tightest (and yes, ebony) styles yet to do a little grocery shopping in Calabasas, Calif., on Thursday, Aug. 27.

    Strolling into the Erewhon Organic Grocer and Cafe, the soon-to-be second-time mama, 34, modeled a scoop-neck LBD with cap sleeves, teamed with strappy sandals. She added Celine shades to shield her eyes from the sun. A gold chain necklace completed the ensemble.

    For glam, the reality star, who already shares 2-year-old daughter North with husband Kanye West, chose bold brows, bronzed skin, and a cocoa-colored lip. She styled her hair into a sleek low bun, which surely helped to keep her cool in L.A.’s soaring temps (90 degrees and up!).

    Naturally, Kardashian was accompanied on the outing by her squad. Both her mom, Kris Jenner (in a long-sleeved, all-black ensemble), and sister Kourtney Kardashian (in an ebony top and Daisy Dukes) joined the Kim Kardashian Hollywood creator for the daytime adventure.
