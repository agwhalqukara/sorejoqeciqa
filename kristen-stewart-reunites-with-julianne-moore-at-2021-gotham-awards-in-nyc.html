    Kristen Stewart Reunites With Julianne Moore at 2021 Gotham Awards in NYC

    Kristen Stewart stuns in a bright pink dress at the 2021 Gotham Awards held at Cipriani Wall Street on Monday (November 29) in New York City. The 31-year-old actress reunited with her Still Alice co-star Julianne Moore on the red carpet, who will be presenting her with the Performer Tribute honor.

    28 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Kristen Stewart stuns in a bright pink dress at the 2021 Gotham Awards held at Cipriani Wall Street on Monday (November 29) in New York City.

    The 31-year-old actress reunited with her Still Alice co-star Julianne Moore on the red carpet, who will be presenting her with the Performer Tribute honor.

    “We are tremendously proud to honor Kristen Stewart, whose superb performance as Princess Diana in Spencer marks yet another artistic peak in a remarkable career which spans some of the most successful studio films and iconic independent films of the past two decades,” Jeffrey Sharp, executive director of the Gotham Film & Media Institute, said in a statement about Kristen‘s accolade.

    He added, “By consistently taking on bold and challenging roles across genres, Kristen has established herself as one of the most respected and beloved artists in the independent film community that we represent here at The Gotham.”

    Kristen and Julianne are also pictured with Dakota Johnson, Tessa Thompson and Spencer director Pablo Larrain at the event.
