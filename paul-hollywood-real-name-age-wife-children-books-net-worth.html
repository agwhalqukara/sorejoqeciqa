    Paul Hollywood’s real name and age

    Paul Hollywood’s real name is Paul John Hollywood, and he was born on March 1, 1966, in Wallasey, Cheshire, England.

    He is currently 57 years old.

    He comes from a family of bakers, as his father owned a bakery chain called Bread Winner.

    Paul learned the art of baking from his father and later studied sculpture at the Wallasey School of Art.

    However, he dropped out of school to pursue his passion for baking.

    Paul Hollywood’s books

    Paul Hollywood is not only a talented baker but also a successful author. He has written several books on baking, including:

    • 100 Great Breads (2004), which won the Gourmand World Cookbook Award for Top Bread and Pastry Book.
    • How to Bake (2012), which covers the basics of baking and features over 120 recipes.
    • Paul Hollywood’s Bread (2013), which showcases his expertise in bread making and offers tips and tricks for different types of bread.
    • Paul Hollywood’s Pies and Puds (2013), which celebrates the British tradition of pies and puddings, with both sweet and savory recipes.
    • Paul Hollywood’s British Baking (2014), which explores the regional specialties and history of British baking, with recipes from all over the UK.
    • The Weekend Baker (2016), which features recipes inspired by his travels around the world, with a focus on easy and delicious bakes for the weekend.
    • A Baker’s Life (2017), which is a memoir and a cookbook, with 100 recipes that reflect his personal and professional journey as a baker.
    • BAKE (2023), which is his latest book and his ultimate collection of bakes, with his best-ever recipes for classic cakes, biscuits, breads, pastries, pizzas, and desserts.

    Paul Hollywood’s wife

    He is currently married to Melissa Spalding, a pub landlady who is 18 years younger than him.

    They tied the knot in a lavish ceremony in Cyprus in September 2023, after getting engaged in November 2022.

    The two had been dating since December 2019, when they spent Christmas together at Melissa’s pub in Kent.

    They moved in together during the lockdown in 2020, and have been inseparable ever since.

    Paul Hollywood was previously married to Alexandra Hollywood, a cookbook author and TV presenter.

    They met in Cyprus in 1996 when he was working as a head baker at a hotel and she was a scuba-diving instructor.

    The two got engaged after a month of dating, and married in 1998.

    They had a son, Josh, in 2001.

    However, their marriage was rocky, and they briefly split in 2013, after Paul admitted to having an affair with his co-star Marcela Valladolid.

    They reconciled, but eventually divorced in 2019, after 19 years of marriage.

    Paul Hollywood also had a relationship with Summer Monteys-Fullam, a barmaid who was 29 years younger than him.

    They met in 2017 when he hired her to organize his wife’s birthday party.

    They started dating shortly after and moved in together in 2018.

    However, they broke up in 2019, after Paul asked her to sign a non-disclosure agreement, which she refused.

    Paul Hollywood’s children

    Paul Hollywood has one child, a son named Josh, who was born on October 1, 2001.

    He is 22 years old and is studying.

    Josh is rarely seen in public with his father and did not attend his wedding to Melissa.

    He is also not active on social media and prefers to keep a low profile.

    Paul has said that he is very proud of his son and that they occasionally bake together.

    Paul Hollywood’s net worth

    He has an estimated net worth of $15 million.

    He earns most of his income from his TV appearances, especially as a judge on The Great British Bake Off, which he has been doing since 2010.

    Paul also makes money from his books, his live shows, his merchandise, and his endorsements.

    He has a luxury lifestyle and owns several properties, cars, and motorcycles.

    Conclusion

    Paul Hollywood is a baking star who has risen to fame and fortune with his skills, his charisma, and his personality.

    He is a judge on one of the most popular TV shows in the world, a best-selling author, and a successful businessman.

    Paul is also a husband, a father, and a lover of life.

    He is a man who has achieved his dreams, and who continues to inspire millions of people with his passion for baking.
