    Early Life and Childhood

    Corde Broadus was born in Long Beach, California, in the United States, on August 21, 1994. As of 2023, he is 29 years old.

    He was born three years before his parents married legally on June 12, 1997, and is the oldest kid of famous rapper Snoop Dogg and his wife, Shante Taylor.

    But in addition to being well-known as a famous child, Corde is also an actress and rapper named Spanky Danky. 

    Moreover, Corde attended Long Beach Polytechnic High School after completing his high school education at Diamond Bar.

    At ten years old, Broadus experienced his parents’ divorce in 2004. Up to their reconciliation in 2008, he and his siblings divided their time between their parents. 

    After that, he grew closer to his father and was influenced by many of his interests, particularly football. So, until 2012, Broadus received professional football training.

    Later, he decided to follow in his father’s footsteps and pursue acting and music. He started off doing some modeling but finally decided to concentrate on his career as a hip-hop musician. Under the name, Kalvin Love has made some records and worked with artists like Big We$ and Planet Falcon, but never his father.
