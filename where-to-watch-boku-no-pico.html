    What is Boku No Pico?

    Boku no Pico is a series of four-panel manga by Midori Endō, published by Ichijinsha. The manga was serialized in the manga magazine Monthly Comic Zero-Sum and currently has 4 volumes released. It follows the life of an elementary school student named Pico and his friends.

    In an interview with Anime News Network about the anime adaptation, Endou said that her inspiration for Boku no Pico was “to depict cute boys having fun at camp”, stating that she wanted to make something different from other yaoi works targeting adult men.[1]
