    Roan Curtis Plays Young Kate Mularkey in the Netflix Series Firefly Lane

    Firefly Lane is based on the book of the same name by acclaimed writer Kristin Hannah. The book was optioned by Netflix and gave the green light for the show on 22 February 2019 with a 10-episode series order. The casting was done soon after and filming began in September 2019 and ended in January 2019 in Burnaby, British Columbia.

Netflix's synopsis reads, "Best friends Tully and Kate support each other through good times and bad with an unbreakable bond that carries them from their teens to their 40s."

    Roan Curtis plays young Kate Mularkey in Firefly Lane. Kate is a smart and unassuming girl who lacks self-confidence and is fine being on her own. She is also a nerd but Kate forms a strong bond with Tully as they become best friends and stick together through thick and thin. Roan's Kate lives in the 1970s.
