    Adam Levine Serenades Behati Prinsloo on First Wedding Anniversary

    Adam Levine just couldnt hold back his feelings for too long, baby. The Maroon 5 crooner got up onstage at Floras Field Kitchen at Flora Farm in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, over the weekend to sing his wife, model Behati Prinsloo, a sweet serenade.

    14 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Adam Levine just couldn’t hold back his feelings for too long, baby. The Maroon 5 crooner got up onstage at Flora’s Field Kitchen at Flora Farm in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, over the weekend to sing his wife, model Behati Prinsloo, a sweet serenade.

    In a video capture of the touching moment, Levine, 36, can be seen soulfully belting the lyrics of Marvin Gaye’s “Let’s Get It On” to Prinsloo, 26, who sits beaming at him just a few feet away.

    The couple, who wed in Mexico last July, were celebrating their one-year wedding anniversary in Cabo — in the same place where they exchanged their vows before 300 guests, including Jonah Hill, Jason Segel, Erin Heatherton, and Candice Swanepoel.

    According to a report from NBC’s KNTV, the pair were having dinner when Levine spontaneously got up onstage to sing to his wife.

    Levine’s romantic gesture comes just as news of his fellow Voice coach Blake Shelton‘s marriage has come to an end — but this isn’t the first time The Voice coach has publicly expressed his love.
