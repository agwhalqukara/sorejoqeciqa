    Hospital gown swag? Nick Cannon gave his fans a troubling update on Wednesday, July 22, Instagramming a photo from a hospital bed.

    “Dont worry, Temporary pit stop… Sometimes I can be a little too bull headed and stubborn,” the America’s Got Talent host, 34, captioned the pic of himself lying down while wearing a black bucket hat. “I gotta stop running my body to the ground. I be wanting to grind nonstop but the engine can't run on fumes.” 

    Mariah Carey’s ex went on to share a theory, noting, “On the low though, I really think I'm a super hero… Now I'm not saying I'm Batman, but I am saying nobody has ever seen me and Batman in the same room together. LOL #ImGood.” 

    Though he didn’t clarify exactly what he’d been hospitalized for, Cannon’s caption indicates his ailing condition had something to do with exhaustion. He was previously hospitalized in 2012 for “mild kidney failure.”
