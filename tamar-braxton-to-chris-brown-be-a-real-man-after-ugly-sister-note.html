    Tamar is talkin' back! The Real co-host Tamar Braxton took to Instagram late Monday, Nov. 3, to respond to Chris Brown's spiteful remarks regarding her physical appearance and Adrienne Bailon's history with the Kardashians.

    On Monday's episode of The Real, Braxton suggested that the co-hosts "pull an inter-friend-tion" with Brown's off-and-on girlfriend Karrueche Tran, 26, while Bailon speculated whether Tran's friends were truly looking out for her best interest.

    "You are asked to talk about certain things," Braxton, 37, wrote on Instagram of hosting a show, citing examples like "A. relationships B. family C. fashion D. Things that has to do with HER in life!"

    Her comments were a direct response to Brown's expletive-filled rant on Instagram about her and Bailon. The "X" singer first targeted Bailon an "ole trout mouth ass bitch," while rehashing her relationship with Rob Kardashian and recent feud with his family members. He also lashed out against Braxton, saying "U take the role of the ugly sister." Brown added to Toni Braxton's younger sibling that her plastic surgery "f—ed yo face up," while calling her a "Muppet face ass!"

"I do not know nor do I give a hot damn about your relationship personally…. honestly and respectfully I'm VERY sorry if you BOTH were offended about our girl chat…. however you WILL NOT think its ok to come at GROWN women and talk to either myself or @adrienne_bailon as if u have EVER has us each in your bed at ANY point in our lives!
