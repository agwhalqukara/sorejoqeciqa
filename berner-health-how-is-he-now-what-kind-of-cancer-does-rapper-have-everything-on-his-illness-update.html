    Berner Health: How Is He Now? What Kind Of Cancer Does Rapper Have? Was It Colon Cancer?

    Berner, a well-known rapper was diagnosed with Colon Cancer. When he was diagnosed, he was in late-stage two, almost stage three.

    The San Francisco rapper announced the news on Wednesday, Oct. 13, 2021, in an Instagram post. He wrote, “I was always transparent and shared my journey with you guys.”

    “So, instead of fighting this alone, I decided to enlist the help of the people who got me to where I am today.

    “Three weeks ago, they discovered cancer traces in a series of blood tests.” I went in for a procedure today hoping to get out of it easy, only to find out I’m in for a major fight.

    Berner then thanked his fans for their support in a tweet the following day.

    He stated, “I’d like to thank everyone for their overwhelming love and support. It helped me more than you can imagine and gave me the energy and motivation I needed to keep fighting.

    I’m ready to karate kick cancer, and I’m so thankful I’m not doing it alone.”

    However, he had never revealed what type of cancer he was fighting with. But in an interview with Breakfast Club Power 105.1 FM on December 3, 2021, he revealed that it was colon cancer that also of late two-stage.

    Rapper Berner Health Update- How Is He Now?
